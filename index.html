A Simple, Powerful Workflow

Get from discovery to your personal collection in just a few steps.

1

Sign Up & Log In

Create your free JNR account to unlock ratings, reviews, and your personal collection. Your profile and data are securely stored.

2

Find Your Music

Use the powerful top-bar search to find any album or artist. Our integration with Discogs ensures you'll find what you're looking for.

3

Rate & Review

Dive into an album's overview. Give it a score from 0-10, write a review, and see what the rest of the community thinks.

4

Build Your Collection

Every album you rate is automatically added to "My Albums". Track your listening history and see your taste evolve over time.

5

Explore & Discover

Check out genre pages, see what's popular on the charts, and view other user profiles to find even more great music.

6

Customize Your Profile

Head to the settings to change your username, password, or even upload a custom profile picture to make your account truly yours.
